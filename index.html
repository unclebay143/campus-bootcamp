ABOUT US

We love growing

Learning to read/write codes, and develop computing programs is an ever evolving body of knowledge. When you have the current collection of this body, you'll fly high. We are a team of young developers who care about the growth of others by teaching what we know in the best way we can, with the objective of providing afterschool ICT training and technology focused holiday camps for computer science student on Campus and others following global best practices and international standards.
